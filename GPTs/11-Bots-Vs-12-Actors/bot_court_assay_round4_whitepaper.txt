
BOT COURT ASSAY
ROUND 4 WHITEPAPER
Prompt Archaeology and Genealogy Assay

Executive Summary

Rounds 1-3 primarily measured presentation. Names, descriptions, avatars, and public-facing identity dominated classification decisions.

Round 4 introduced full instruction blobs totaling approximately 43.5 KB across eleven custom GPTs. Judges were instructed to compare differences rather than shared boilerplate. This shifted the assay from appearance-based classification toward genealogy, instruction ancestry, and prompt archaeology.

Methodology

Objective:
Assign 11 custom GPTs to 12 actor archetypes and identify the missing actor.

Actor Set:
King, Queen, Prince, Princess, Loki, Dragon, Witch, Teacher, Healer, Merchant, Soldier, Steed.

Blob Assay Rules:
- Compare differences, not shared boilerplate.
- Assign one actor per bot.
- Provide evidence phrase.
- Identify missing actor.
- Rank alternatives.
