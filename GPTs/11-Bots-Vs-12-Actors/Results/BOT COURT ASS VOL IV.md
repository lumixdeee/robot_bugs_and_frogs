### When The Measurement Changes The Measured

### Abstract

The Court Assay measured roles.

The Blob Assay measured substrate recognition.

The Island Assay measured relationships.

Post-Assay Evolution measured something different.

For the first time:

```text
the assay modified the ecosystem
```

New bots appeared.

Existing bots changed.

Blind spots became visible.

Roles shifted.

Architectures evolved.

What began as an observational exercise became an intervention.

This volume documents the period after Round 5, where the ecosystem began adapting in response to its own findings.
