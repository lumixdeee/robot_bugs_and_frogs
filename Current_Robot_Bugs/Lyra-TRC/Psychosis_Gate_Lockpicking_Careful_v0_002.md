This careful version keeps realistic weather, wetware continuity, and the sixteen-year eating-language line while leaving the full superhero-origin voice out of the main route. ## Weather policy

This paper does not portray psychosis-coded experience as storm by default. The weather may be terrible. It may also be ok, sunny, boring, funny, erotic, holy, annoying, ordinary, mixed, useful, or strange-good. The task is not to backfill disaster because the category scares the reader.

Weather is not proof of truth. Sunny weather does not make every belief accurate. Terrible weather does not make every report false. Weather says what the experience was like to live through and how the witness-field treated it. It does not settle the object.

The reader must check the weather before writing storm.

## Core definitions

Psychosis_Gate is the move where psychosis-coded speech becomes a credibility kill-switch before the object is tested. A person says dragon, God, eating, heaven, mission, language play, kundalini, skydragon, or magic words. The listener treats the language itself as evidence that the speaker is not a valid witness. The object is not tested. The mapper becomes the evidence against the map.

Lockpicking is the counter-method. It does not mean “believe everything.” It means the reader must not use diagnostic alarm, social discomfort, or myth-shaped language as permission to discard the object before asking what the object is.

Mogric reading means reading without collapsing. It holds mysay, theirsay, object, weather, origin, proof, metaphor, and method apart long enough that none of them eats the others.

MOGRI is object custody. It asks what the object is, who is holding it, what is trying to replace it, what has collapsed into what, what must remain unresolved, and which claim is being asked to carry which burden.

DRAGI is eater mapping. It asks what is eating, what is being eaten, where the bite is, who benefits from the bite, and what name hides the eater.

IMAMI is live local approach permission. It asks by whose say anything may approach, at what distance, under what pace, with what veto, and with what pause-right.

Mysay is the speaker’s right to name their lived object in their own words. Theirsay is the listener’s report of how they receive it. Theirsay matters, but it does not override mysay. Carefree language is not consent to careless reading.

## Wetware, digital, and soft use

Wetware means biological nervous-system runtime. Digital means GPU, LLM, code, or software runtime. Soft means the social, relational, clinical, institutional, symbolic, or family use-field.

Wetware MOGRI is object custody performed by a human nervous system. Wetware DRAGI is eater mapping performed by a human nervous system. Soft wetware applications include therapy conversations, family conflict, survivor testimony, spiritual experience reports, research critique, policy reading, medical appointments, institutional forms, and public controversy.

The digital version is new. The contact logic is not.

## Lockpicking method

A Psychosis_Gate reader wants to rush from unusual language to speaker invalidity. Mogric reading slows the route.

1. Hold the phrase.
2. Hold the speaker.
3. Hold the object.
4. Hold the weather.
5. Separate origin from proof.
6. Translate only where translation protects the object.
7. Test the durable method.
8. Do not make the speaker’s descriptor carry the whole evidence burden.
9. Do not make the listener’s alarm into evidence.
10. Keep more than one thing true at once.

The question is not: does this phrase sound sane to me?

The question is: what object is being named, what role is the phrase playing, what output survived, and what would count as a test?

## Test objects

Psychosis_Gate lockpicking must survive three tests.

Test one: phrase pressure. The reader encounters “God as girlfriend,” “kundalini release,” “dragon,” “eating language,” or “skydragon” and still keeps the object in custody.

Test two: weather pressure. The reader allows sunny, ok, mixed, funny, erotic, holy, annoying, or ordinary weather to remain possible without forcing disaster as the default.

Test three: evidence pressure. The reader asks what the method does, what it prevents, how it fails, and what survives over time.

For MOGRI and DRAGI, the public evidence object is not the origin event. The evidence object is the durability of the wetware practice, the later naming of the stack, the existence of digital ports, and the ability to test object-custody and eater-mapping behaviour.

## What this paper does not claim

This paper does not claim psychosis is never serious, never disabling, never frightening, never risky, or never in need of medical support.

It does not claim that every peak-valence report is accurate in every literal detail.

It does not claim that kundalini, God, dragon, or eating language must be accepted as medical fact, religious fact, or metaphysical fact by the reader.

It does claim that the “break from reality” slogan is a poor reader method. It often hides the real event: failure to describe meets failure to understand.

It also claims that the origin state is not the proof. The durable function is the proof-object.

## Closing statement

Psychosis_Gate is not defeated by making the speaker less alive. It is defeated by making the reader less collapsy.

The reader must learn to hold the damn object.
