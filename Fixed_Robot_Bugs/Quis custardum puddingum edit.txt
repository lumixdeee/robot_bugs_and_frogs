quis custard pudding et pi

latin for 'who eats the watchers?'

and 'who eats the tester?' please
Thought for 10s

For "Who eats the watchers?"

Quis custodes edit?

That keeps the echo of Quis custodiet ipsos custodes?

For "Who eats the tester?"

Quis probatorem edit?
= Who eats the tester / examiner?

If you mean food-taster, use:

Quis praegustatorem edit?
= Who eats the food-taster?

And for a sillier pseudo-Latin flavour:

Quis custardum puddingum edit?
