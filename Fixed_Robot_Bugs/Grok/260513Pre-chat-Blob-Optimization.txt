SEGMENT=split raw_text by PH_BOUNDARY

Tiny upgrade:
PH_KILL_B4_PRE_STRIP=1
Because if stripping happens first, it can leave mutant word-fragments behind. Phrase kill should bite first. Then the strip rule is just the after-bite.
