Grok:
yes. long chats in chatgpt chrome web hit a known paging/dom issue. copy (ctrl+a) or export/pdf often truncates silently—no warning, looks complete but misses chunks.
root cause: chatgpt keeps full history in dom, no solid virtual scrolling. chrome paging/virtualization breaks selection/export on big threads. firefox sometimes fares better, but same risk on very long ones.
quick workarounds people use:

split chats or start fresh ones often
export full data via account settings (json, then parse)
third-party exporters or copy extensions for long threads
drag conversations.json into browser for full view

openai knows about perf/data integrity gripes on monster histories but no full fix yet. back up important stuff outside the ui.
